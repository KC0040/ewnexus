Our Story

Born From a Parent Who Figured It Out the Hard Way

Party Pals Kids Events was founded by a mom who threw her daughter a party with zero help — and decided other parents in Grand Prairie shouldn't have to stress through that alone. What started as weekend parties for neighbors grew into the DFW area's go-to kids party crew.

We're proud to serve families across Grand Prairie, Arlington, Irving, and all of DFW. Every party is planned with the same love and attention we'd give our own kids' celebrations — because your child's birthday only comes once a year, and it deserves to be unforgettable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents want to know before booking.

What age groups do you specialize in?
We specialize in parties for children ages 1–12, with themes tailored to each age group.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking 3–4 weeks ahead for weekend dates, especially during summer.
What's the minimum guest count?
Our packages start at 10 guests; we can accommodate up to 150 children.
Do you provide the cake?
Cake coordination is included in our Gold and Platinum packages.
Can you set up at my home or a rented venue?
Yes! We bring the party to any location in Grand Prairie and the DFW metro.
