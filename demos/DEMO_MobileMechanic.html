Our Story

Built in Grand Prairie, Built for DFW

RoadReady Mobile Mechanic was founded in 2017 by Marcus Delgado, an ASE-certified technician who spent 12 years in dealership bays and decided the future of auto repair was coming to the customer — not the other way around. Marcus grew up in Grand Prairie and knows every highway, side street, and parking structure from Arlington to Irving.

Today, RoadReady serves hundreds of drivers and small fleets across the DFW metro. We're the first call for property managers, delivery companies, and everyday drivers who can't afford downtime. Our van is stocked with diagnostic equipment, common repair parts, and everything needed to get you rolling — fast.
