Our Story

Grand Prairie's Mobile Tire Experts

SpeedRim Mobile Tire was founded in 2019 by Diana Ruiz, a former tire shop manager who saw firsthand how much time customers wasted sitting in waiting rooms. Diana's idea was simple: put the equipment in a van, hire certified techs, and let customers keep their day while we do the work. SpeedRim launched with a single van and a text-based booking system built on a whiteboard.

Five years later, SpeedRim operates two fully-equipped mobile units covering Grand Prairie, Arlington, Irving, and surrounding DFW communities. Our GPS-tracked dispatch system lets customers monitor their tech in real time — something not even the big tire chains can offer. We've become the first call for property managers, corporate fleet accounts, and anyone who knows their time is worth more than a waiting room chair.
