Common Questions

Rental FAQ

Everything you need to know before you reserve. Still have questions? Give us a call.

How far in advance should I reserve?
We recommend booking 1–2 weeks ahead for peak weekends and holidays. Summer weekends and holiday weekends fill up fast — especially for tents, kayaks, and party setups. The earlier the better for large events.
What's the deposit requirement?
A 25% deposit is required to confirm your reservation; balance due at pickup. The deposit is collected via our online form or over the phone. It guarantees your equipment is held for your rental dates.
What if equipment is damaged during my rental?
A damage waiver is available for a small fee. See our rental agreement for full details. We strongly recommend the waiver for high-activity rentals like kayaks, tents, and generators. Normal wear is expected — the waiver covers accidental damage.
Do you deliver?
Yes, delivery and pickup available within 30 miles of Grand Prairie for a small fee. Delivery is a great option for large events — we'll drop off tents, tables, and chairs and pick everything up when you're done. Call us to schedule.
Can I extend my rental on-site?
Extensions are possible subject to availability — call us to confirm before your return date. We'll do our best to accommodate, especially on weekdays. Peak weekend extensions may not be available if the item is reserved by another customer.
