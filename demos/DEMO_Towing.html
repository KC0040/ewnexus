Our Story

Grand Prairie's Most Trusted Towing Team

Lone Star Towing was founded in 2015 by Ray Castellano, a veteran roadside operator who spent years driving for the Texas Department of Transportation incident response fleet. Ray started with one truck and a philosophy: when someone calls for help, you treat it like a family emergency — because for them, it is.

Today, Lone Star runs a fleet of 4 tow trucks covering Grand Prairie, Arlington, and the entire DFW metro. We're the backup provider for three local dealerships, handle overflow dispatch for two roadside assistance networks, and have earned a reputation for arriving when we say we will — even at 2am in a rainstorm.
