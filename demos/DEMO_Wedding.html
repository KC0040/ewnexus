Common Questions

Wedding Planning FAQ

We believe in transparency from the start. Here are the questions every couple asks — answered honestly.

How far in advance should I book?
Popular dates fill 12–18 months ahead. We recommend booking as early as possible — especially for spring and fall dates in DFW, which tend to go quickly. That said, we do occasionally have openings on shorter timelines. Contact us and we'll let you know what's available.
Do you offer day-of coordination only?
Yes — we offer full planning, partial planning, and day-of coordination packages. Day-of coordination is perfect for couples who have planned everything themselves and just need a trusted professional to execute on the day. We'll take over 4–6 weeks before your wedding to review all vendor contracts and timelines.
Can you work with vendors I've already booked?
Absolutely — we coordinate seamlessly with your existing vendors. We've worked with hundreds of DFW vendors and are comfortable integrating with whomever you've chosen. We'll collect their contact info, confirm timelines, and make sure everyone is aligned well before your wedding day.
What's included in a full-service package?
Venue scouting, vendor management, florals coordination, timeline, and day-of coordination. Full-service also includes unlimited consultations throughout the planning process, budget management, contract review, rehearsal direction, and complete day-of execution from setup to last dance.
Do you plan events outside DFW?
We primarily serve DFW but can travel for destination weddings — ask about our travel packages. We've planned weddings in Austin, San Antonio, New Mexico, and Colorado. Travel packages include accommodation and a destination planning fee — ask us for a custom quote.
