// Data Flow & Security

Where Does Customer Data Go?

Short answer: your customer data goes to your server, your Telegram, and nowhere else.

storage
Stored on your Hostinger server
Form submissions are saved to your own database on your own hosting account. EWNexus does not have ongoing access to your customer data after launch.
send
Sent to your Telegram only
Notifications go to your personal Telegram account (or a private group of your choosing). No one else sees them.
smart_toy
AI conversations — US-based only
The AI uses Anthropic Claude (American company, servers in the US). We explicitly do not use Chinese AI services (DeepSeek, etc.) due to data residency concerns. Anthropic's privacy policy prohibits using API conversations for model training by default.
block
We don't sell or share your data. Ever.
Your customer list is yours. We have no business relationship with advertisers, data brokers, or third-party marketers. Read our full privacy policy →
