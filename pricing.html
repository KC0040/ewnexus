// Pricing FAQ

A page = a unique URL with its own purpose. It's not about scroll sections — a "Services" section on your homepage is not a page. A page is something like /services, /menu, /about, /contact.

Standard 5 pages (included in $500): Home · About · Services or Menu · Contact · One industry-specific page (Gallery, FAQ, Portfolio, Booking Info, etc.)

Counts as extra pages (+$100 each): Each individual service detail page (e.g. /ac-repair, /heating) · Each location page · Blog posts · Product listing pages · Online store category/product pages

Not a page: Sections within a page · Contact forms · Popups · Sliders · Google Maps embeds
